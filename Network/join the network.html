Opportunities for networking

The AKMG Global has a global network of members that offers a forum for Kerala-born medical and dental professionals to connect, work together, and exchange knowledge and expertise.

Cultural exchange

The AKMG Global plans social and cultural events to connect its members with their ancestry and to promote Kerala's rich heritage and traditions.

Advocacy

AKMG Global fights for the interests of the healthcare industry as well as the rights and welfare of medical and dental professionals.

Professional advancement

AKMG Global provides initiatives and programs to support the growth and development of members' careers. The group offers opportunities for CME credits and staying current with medical advancements.

Philanthropy connect

Social Connect

AKMG Global supports charitable endeavors enabling like-minded doctors to join together.

The Association of Medical and Dental Doctors of Kerala (AKMG Global) is a global networking platform that unites medical and dental professionals of Kerala origin from across the world.

A person with ancestral or cultural roots in the Indian state of Kerala is considered of Kerala origin. Keralites reside globally for professional and personal reasons.

Members gain access to global networking opportunities, educational resources, professional collaborations, events, and the AKMG Global online community.

Membership is open to actively practicing medical and dental professionals of Kerala origin.

AKMG Global operates as a pan-global virtual network and does not charge a basic membership fee.
