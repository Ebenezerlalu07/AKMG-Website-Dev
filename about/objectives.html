About AKMG Global

About AKMG Global

The Association of Kerala Medical and Dental Graduates (AKMG) Global is a global networking platform that unites medical and dental professionals with Keralan origin from all over the world.

Medical or Dental Doctors from Kerala have established themselves in communities and countries across the globe. They have risen to the highest echelons of the medical domain in their adopted countries, as researchers, teachers, policy makers, entrepreneurs, and superb medical practitioners. Their innate resourcefulness, diagnostic and problem-solving minds, and culture of service have enabled them to succeed in the countries they have chosen to serve.

The vast numbers of doctors practicing in countries such as the US, Canada, Australia, the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and beyond present a unique opportunity to create a powerful networking community. This is a collaborative effort of like-minded doctors with the goal of bringing together their brethren across the world under a single umbrella.

AKMG Global aims to strengthen these bonds and create a vibrant community of healthcare professionals who can support each other in their personal and professional endeavours. Many of our colleagues have the capability and resources to initiate professional and academic exchanges for the benefit of society, to communities and locations where they are needed. Our shared language and culture, which resonates even with our families and children, provide a strong foundation for this connection.

Objectives of AKMG Global


Networking : The organization facilitates the member associates to connect, work towards common goals, and exchange knowledge and expertise. This may require creating expert support online channels to for the benefit of the society for easily dissemination of accurate and credible information regarding issues pertaining to health and disease thereby propagating the practice of ethical and evidence-based scientific medicine among its members.

Professional Development : Encouraging research in medical and dental sciences with grants, out of the funds of the association through the establishment of scholarships, prizes, awards, and rewards. Organizing seminars, educational programs, and publish scientific journals to keep its members up-to-date with the latest developments in medicine and dentistry. Promoting the knowledge, study, and practice of medicine and dentistry in all aspects, as well as to advance educational and scientific development among its

Missions and Advocacy : AKMG Global stands for the interests of the healthcare sector as well as the rights and welfare of medical and dental professionals. Advocacy involves creating and sustaining a channel of communication and dialogue with official bodies in India and abroad for the betterment of the profession and to safeguard members' professional rights in the society. Also to coordinate with the Indian Medical Association (IMA), Kerala State and support IMA's activities whenever possible.

Philanthropy : Organize services of humanitarian and social service nature all over the world. Provide preventive healthcare and medical and dental assistance to communities worldwide, especially in Kerala and extend humanitarian services to people in need.

Social and Cultural :  Promote cooperation and friendship among its members and their families, foster their cultural, social, and personal development, also intended to promote Kerala's rich heritage and traditions.
